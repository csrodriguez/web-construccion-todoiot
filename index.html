Description

This is the first version, under construction, of a custom platform to manage IoT devices, and that can be implemented both on own servers or in the cloud.

This project includes both front-end and back-end development, with all the necessary services (MQTT, databases, and others). In addition, it also includes the development of custom IoT devices (based on ESPxx, among others).
